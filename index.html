Understanding Podiatric Medicine

Podiatric medicine is a specialized field focused on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of foot and ankle disorders. Podiatrists, also known as chiropodists, undergo extensive training to provide expert care for a wide range of conditions. From minor ailments to complex surgeries, podiatrists are equipped to handle it all.
